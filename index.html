About

WCO JFB Night Shift Calculator

Calculating night shifts means keeping track of multiple allowances across different shift types, distances, and schedules — and doing that mental arithmetic at the end of a long period is tedious and error-prone.

The WCO JFB Night Shift Calculator is a simple tool built by L/Cpl. R. Marshall for the Portland Division and any other JFB personnel to calculate their night shift allowances quickly and accurately. Instead of manually tallying up your SP1, SP2, Meal, and Taxi allowance, you enter your shift numbers and the app does the rest — giving you a clear breakdown and total in seconds.

What it calculates

  • SP1 — your 3PM shift allowance
  • SP2 — your 10PM shift allowance
  • Meal — meal allowance based on total shifts worked
  • Taxi — transport allowance adjusted for distance, shift count, and 10PM cancellations

Who it's for

This app is for JFB members who need to calculate their night shift. Whether you work short or long distance routes, or a mix of both, the calculator handles standard and advanced calculations in one place.

How to get started

Open the app, enter your shift numbers for the period, select your distance, and tap Calculate. Use Advanced mode if you have a mix of short and long distance shifts. Add it to your home screen for quick access between shifts.

How to Use

Standard

🕒

Total 3PM Shifts — Enter the total number of 3PM shifts worked this period, including regular, extra, and exchanges.

🕙

Total 10PM Shifts — Enter the total number of 10PM shifts worked, including regular, extra, and exchanges.

📅

Amount of 10PM Cancellations (after your 3PM Shift) — Enter the number of days where your 10PM days leave was cancelled after working a 3PM shift. This deducts 2 from the taxi count per day.

📍

Distance — Select Short ($950/shift) or Long ($2,000/shift) for your taxi allowance rate.

Advanced

⚡️

Advanced — Use this if you have a mix of short and long distance shifts in the same period. Enter your short and long distance shifts separately. The calculator combines both into a single set of results.

Install

📲

Add to Home Screen — On iPhone, tap the Share button in Safari and select "Add to Home Screen". On Android, tap the menu in Chrome and select "Add to Home Screen". The app will run fullscreen just like a native app, and works offline.
